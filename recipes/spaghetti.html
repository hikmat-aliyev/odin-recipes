Spaghetti Recipe

Description

If you're wondering what the secret is to a good spaghetti, here's a hint: it's all about the sauce! Whether it's a meat sauce, marinara, or creamy cheese sauce, you'll want to be sure the noodles are fully incorporated into the sauce. Some of the recipes ahead even call for cooking the pasta in the sauce, like the slow cooker spaghetti with spicy sausage. Then, you can whip up your favorite garlic bread for the ultimate Italian experience. With these pasta recipes, you'll be on your way to dinner served in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb. Italian tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 7 ounces evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add spaghetti to the water
  3. Fry onions for 10 minutes
  4. Add tomato sauce to the onion
  5. Boil the spaghetti for 20 minutes
  6. Add milk to the sauce after 10 minutes
  7. Add the spaghetti to the sauce
  8. Add spaghetti water to the sauce
  9. Boil the spaghetti for 5 minutes
